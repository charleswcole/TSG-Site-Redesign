FAQs

Questions?

You've got questions and we have answers! Please look over our frequently asked questions to the right. If you have a question that isn't answered here, click this link and ask us now!
What kind of savings can we expect from TSG?

Last year, savings for our customers ranged from 15 to 50%. It is important to remember every company’s situation is unique. In order to determine the savings that your organization may secure, TSG will perform a FREE transportation analysis. This process includes a thorough audit of current shipping needs, patterns and costs as well as a projection of savings you may receive on an annual basis utilizing a customized program developed by TSG.

Is my motor carrier service affected?

Absolutely. Service levels are IMPROVED through your relationship with TSG because we are prepared to recommend carriers that best suit your unique needs. We’re dedicated to eliminating transportation waste and poor service that you might be experiencing.

Which carriers are used?

There are many potential candidates. In fact, there are over a thousand potential carriers in North America. Ultimately though, you select the carriers your company will use. Our role is to evaluate and recommend the core carriers that are a good fit for your customized program and pass muster with our high standards.

Will we be able to continue to use my current carriers?

You are in full control. We offer freight company recommendations based upon pricing, service and capabilities to meet your needs. Our job is to reduce costs for you. If we can do that with your current carrier, let’s do it.

What does it cost? Are there additional fees?

Our fees are based on your savings. The transportation analysis is completely FREE and there are no upfront costs following it. This means the customized program we develop for your company is completed at no additional charge. The key for us is to use our expertise in the industry to pinpoint waste and negotiate the best rates possible for your operation. The cost to begin is zero; the savings to be gained are exponential in the future.

Does TSG serve my area?

Our customers are located throughout North America. Whether your needs are in the U.S.A., Canada or Mexico, we are fully prepared to meet your needs.
