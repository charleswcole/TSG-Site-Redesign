Logging Into The TSG TMS

This is a quick tutorial on Logging in the TSG TMS

Web Address

To reach the TSG TMS. Click the following Link: TSG TMS

Logging In

Enter your Username and the Password TSG Provided you. Then press the Login Button.

Resetting Passwords

Forget your password? Use the Forgot Password? link. Fill out the form and TSG will email you a new password.

Using Rate Shop

Using the TSG Rate Shop allows you to quickly retrieve the least cost carrier for a particular lane and save that as a spot quote.

Filling Out The Form

The Information you will need to enter into the form:

  • Origin Zip
  • Destination Zip
  • Pickup Date
  • Payment Terms
  • Quantity
  • Packing Type
  • Freight Class
  • Total Weight
  • Dimensions (Optional)

Adding Services

To Provide you with an accurate rate quote, you may need to add special services to the shipment.

Getting Rate Results and Saving a Quote Sheet

Once you fill out the form and submit it, the system will return a list of available carriers for this shipment. You are also able to save these results by pressing the PDF button.
